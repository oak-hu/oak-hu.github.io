##Contextualism
**What is the difference between contextualism and
subject-sensitive invariantism? Which is better motivated by
shifting patterns of knowledge ascriptions?**

*(January 2025): Linguistic judgments differ a lot, but I was surprised to find that mine were so contextualist. Daniel said that he was quite annoyed that he couldn't come up with an example context where 'they ate the snacks. Well, they didn't *eat* them, but they ate them' worked (but finally did come up with the snacks being soup).*
